Tempest (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is a personal productivity application for macOS that helps users manage tasks, focus sessions, and synchronize events with Google Calendar. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using Tempest.

1. Information We Collect
Tempest collects only the minimum data required to provide Google authentication and enable synchronization with Google Calendar.
1.1 Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive the following data through Google’s OpenID Connect service:
Your Google account ID (sub)


Your email address


Your name


Your profile picture URL


This information is used solely to identify you within the App and to associate your data with your Google account.
 Tempest does not access any other Google account information.
1.2 Google Calendar Data
If you grant the Calendar scope (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar), Tempest may:
Read your calendar events


Create new events


Modify existing events


Delete events


Tempest does not access any Google service other than Google Calendar.

2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:
2.1 Authentication
Tempest uses Google Sign-In to verify your identity and allow you to securely access your data without managing passwords.
2.2 Calendar Synchronization
Tempest uses your Google Calendar data to:
Display upcoming events


Synchronize tasks or focus sessions as calendar events


Update calendar events when modified inside Tempest


Delete calendar events when corresponding items are removed in the App


Tempest never edits unrelated calendar data.
2.3 User Experience
Your name and profile picture may be shown inside the App to personalize your experience.

3. How Your Information Is Stored
Tempest does not upload any personal data to external servers.
All authentication tokens (access token, refresh token, ID token) are stored:
Locally on your Mac


Inside the macOS Keychain, protected by the operating system


Never transmitted to any server other than Google’s official OAuth endpoints


Tempest does not store any personal data on cloud servers or third-party services.

4. How Long We Keep Your Information
Tempest only retains the data necessary for Google Calendar synchronization.
Tokens remain stored locally while you are signed in.


When you select Sign Out, all tokens are permanently deleted from the Keychain.


No additional copies are stored anywhere else.



5. Your Data Rights and Control
5.1 Revoke Access
You may revoke Tempest’s access to your Google account at any time by visiting:
 https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
5.2 Delete Local Data
Signing out from Tempest removes all stored authentication tokens from your device.
5.3 Contact the Developer
If you have questions or concerns, you may contact us using the email at the bottom of this document.

6. Children’s Privacy
Tempest is not intended for children under 13 years old.
 We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children.

7. Sharing of Information
Tempest does not:
Sell your personal information


Share your information with any third-party


Use your data for advertising or tracking


Collect analytics or behavioral data


Your information remains private between you and the App.

8. Security
We take security seriously:
All tokens are securely stored in macOS Keychain


Tempest does not use external servers or cloud databases


All communication with Google services is encrypted using HTTPS

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
 Updates will always be available at the public link used to access this document.
